Help Center

What are your opening hours?
Our ice cream parlor is open every day from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
Do you offer dairy-free options?
Yes, we offer a variety of dairy-free and vegan ice cream options made from coconut milk and other plant-based ingredients.
Can I book a birthday party at your parlor?
Absolutely! We'd love to host your birthday party. Please contact us in advance to discuss your requirements and make a reservation.
Do you offer catering services?
Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. Whether it's a corporate gathering or a wedding, we can provide delicious ice cream treats to make your event extra special.
Are your ice creams made in-house?
Yes, all our ice creams are made fresh in-house using the finest ingredients.
Do you have gluten-free options?
Yes, we have a selection of gluten-free ice cream flavors available. Please ask our staff for assistance.
